About Me

This is what I do.

I am currently a second year student at Imperial College London, studying a joint honours degree in Maths and Computer Science. Leaving home and moving to London proved to be one of the most challenging and rewarding things I have ever done.

I was born in Edinburgh, Scotland, in 1999, where I lived in a neighbouring town called Musselburgh which is where I spent most of my childhood. Musselburgh is a reasonably sized town on the south bank of the forth with a long history of fishing, and golf (we actually have the world's oldest golf course).

This blog was mainly a chance for me to get my hands dirty with some front-end web development as I haven't done much until now, and was keen to try and learn some new skills and put them into practise - so hopefully the website will be slowly improving as time goes on.
